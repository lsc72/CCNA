Figure 1 on this page shows an image of wire cutters. Wire cutters are used to cut wires.Figure 2 on this page shows an image of wire strippers. A wire stripper is used to remove the insulation from wire so that it can be twisted to other wires or crimped to connectors to make a cable.Figure 3 on this page shows an image of a crimper. A crimper is used to attach connectors to wires.Figure 4 on this page shows an image of a punch down tool. A punch down tool is used to terminate wire into termination blocks.
