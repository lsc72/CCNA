Figure 1 on this page is an image showing severe dust build-up on a computer component.Figure 2 on this page is a table listing the following items that you should clean and the cleaning materials to use. Computer case and outside of monitor, clean with a mild cleaning solution and a lint-free cloth. LCD screen, clean with LCD cleaning solution or distilled water and a lint-free cloth. CRT screen, clean with distilled water and a lint-free cloth. Heat sink, clean with compressed air. RAM, clean with isopropyl alcohol and a lint-free swab. Keyboard, clean with a hand held vacuum cleaner with a brush attachment. Mouse, clean with a mild cleaning solution and a lint-free cloth.
