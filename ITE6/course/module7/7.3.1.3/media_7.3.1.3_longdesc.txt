Figure 1 on this page is a topology of a LAN. The topology shows three desktop computers, a server, a printer, and a wireless access point connected to a central switch. Three laptops are connected to the LAN via the wireless access point. Figure 2 on this page is a topology that shows two LANs connected over the Internet. The first LAN is a small office network with a printer, desktop computer, and an IP phone are connected to an integrated router by cable. A laptop, a tablet, and a smartphone are connected to the integrated router using wireless. The integrated router is connected to the Internet. The second LAN is a Data centre network with three servers connected to a switch. The switch is connected to a router. The router is connected to the internet.
