The figure on this page is a topology that shows how the following hardware firewall works. An internal network consisting of hosts H1 and H2 and a web server are connected to a central Ethernet switch, which connects all wired hosts. The switch is connected to an internal firewall. An external network, consisting of host H3 connects to the internal router, which routes packets for all hosts. The internal router is connected to the internal firewall, which protects hosts H1 and H2.
