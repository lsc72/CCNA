The figure on this page shows two topologies that illustrate the following two modes of how data can flow through the network. The first topology represents Half-Duplex. It shows 2 desktop computer that are connected. Both desktop computers have a callout that says, “I can send and receive, but not at the same time.” The second topology represents Full-Duplex. It shows 2 desktop computer that are connected. The both computers have a callout that says, “We can send and receive at the same time.”
