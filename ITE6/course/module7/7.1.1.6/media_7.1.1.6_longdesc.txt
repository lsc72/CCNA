The figure on this page shows the following images that represent bandwidth. Bandwidth is like the number of lanes, the first image shows a two lane highway, the second image shows a four lane highway, the third image shows a six lane highway. Network devices are like on-ramps, traffic signals, signs, and maps, the first image shows a highway on-ramp, the second image shows a traffic signal, the third image shows a stop sign and a lane indicator sign, the fourth image shows a map. Travelling data is like travelling vehicles, the first image shows a van, the second image shows a truck, the third image shows a sports car.
