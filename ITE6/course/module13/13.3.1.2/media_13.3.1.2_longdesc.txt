The figure on this page lists the following information that the level one technician must obtain: contact information, what is the manufacture and model of computer? What OS is the computer using? Is the computer plugged in to the wall or running on battery power? Is the computer on a network? If so, is it a wired or wireless connection?  Was any specific applications being used when the problem occurred? Have any new drivers or updates been installed recently? If so, what are they? Description of the problem, priority of problem.
