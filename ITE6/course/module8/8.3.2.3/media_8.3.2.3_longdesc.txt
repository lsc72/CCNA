Figure 1 on this page is a topology that shows a client and a HTTP server connected to a network. Above the network is the domain name, ‘http://www.cisco.com’. Figure 2 on this page is the same topology as figure 1. The address bar of the browser on the client is magnified to show the domain name, ‘http://www.cisco.com’.
