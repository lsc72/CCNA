The figure on this page is a table that lists step 3 in troubleshooting network problems, test the theory to determine cause. Common steps to determine cause: Check that all cables are connected to the proper locations, unseat and then reconnect cables and connectors, reboot the computer or network device, login as a different user, repair or re-enable the network connections, contact the network administrator, ping your default gateway, access a remote web page such as http://www.cisco.com.
