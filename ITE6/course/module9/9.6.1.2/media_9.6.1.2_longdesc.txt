The figure on this page is a table that lists step 2 in troubleshooting laptop and mobile device problems, establish a theory of probable cause. Common causes of laptop problems: Battery does not have a charge, battery will not charge, loose cable connections, keyboard does not work, Num Lock key is on, loose RAM module. Common causes of mobile device problems: The Power button is broken, the battery can no longer hold a charge, there is excessive dirt in the speaker, microphone, or charging port, the mobile device has been dropped, the mobile device has been submerged.
